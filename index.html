About the Project

Degen Pet Trading Floor is a simulation game that combines virtual pets with algorithmic trading strategies. Players can adopt different pets, each with unique trading algorithms, and compete to earn the most $dgpt (Degen Pet Trading Tokens).

This project was created as an educational tool to help understand different trading strategies in a fun and interactive way. The market data is simulated but follows realistic patterns based on historical price action.

The game includes features like:

  • Various trading pets with different strategies
  • Multiple simulated markets
  • Badge and achievement system
  • Virtual economy with consumables
  • Mood system affecting trading performance

Future updates may include additional pets, markets, and strategies. Contributions are welcome!
